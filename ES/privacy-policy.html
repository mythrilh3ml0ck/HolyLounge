Introduction

Holy Lounge is a spiritual and informational application under The Lounge platform, designed to present religious, inspirational, and faith-based content for reflection, guidance, and peaceful viewing.

Information Collection

Holy Lounge does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from users. No registration, login, or personal data input is required.

Device Permissions

Holy Lounge does not request access to sensitive device features such as:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts
  • Storage of personal files

The application operates without accessing private user data.

Internet Usage

The application may use an internet connection to display religious texts, inspirational content, or spiritually themed materials under The Lounge ecosystem. All content is provided for informational and reflective purposes only.

Third-Party Services

Holy Lounge does not use advertising networks, analytics tracking, or third-party data collectors.

Children’s Privacy

Since no personal data is collected, Holy Lounge is safe for users of all ages, including children under parental guidance.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
